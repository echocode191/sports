About Soka Mashinani

Nurturing Football Talents

Soka Mashinani is a movement dedicated to developing local football through nurturing talents at the grassroots level.

We believe that every talented player deserves access to quality gear that can help them reach their full potential.

Our mission is to provide affordable, high-quality football equipment to players across the country.

With our commitment to excellence and community development, we're not just selling football gear - we're investing in the future of Kenyan football.
